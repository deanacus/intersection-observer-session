What the problem?

We need to lazy load our images. We all know the benefits of lazy loading - fast initial loads, not downloading images if they're never on screen, better SEO, etc, etc, etc...

So lets hook up a scroll listener

No!

Scroll listeners are a PITA. They require maths that I can never get 100% right, they run on the main thread which can cause serious performance issues, and when you need more than one, they can bring your page to a crawl on scroll.

What's our alternative?

Intersection Observer!
